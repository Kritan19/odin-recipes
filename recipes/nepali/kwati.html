KWATI-RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

  • Mixed beans (black-eyed peas, kidney beans, chickpeas, black beans, green gram, lentils, etc.): 1 cup (soaked overnight)
  • Chopped onion: 1 medium-sized
  • Chopped garlic: 3 cloves
  • Chopped ginger: 1 tablespoon
  • Cumin seeds: 1 teaspoon
  • Turmeric powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Garam masala powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Chopped tomatoes: 2 medium-sized
  • Vegetable or chicken broth: 6 cups
  • Butter or oil: 2 tablespoons
  • Salt to taste

Method

  1. Rinse the soaked mixed beans thoroughly and set them aside.
  2. In a pot, heat butter/oil. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onion, garlic, and ginger. Saut√© until onions are translucent.
  4. Stir in turmeric powder and garam masala powder.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  6. Add the mixed beans and vegetable or chicken broth. Bring it to a boil.
  7. Once boiling, reduce the heat and simmer until the beans are tender.
  8. Mash a portion of the beans to thicken the broth slightly.
  9. Season with salt according to taste.
  10. Serve hot Kwati soup with rice or as a standalone dish.
