SEL ROTI RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

  • Rice flour: 2 cups
  • Ripe bananas: 2, mashed
  • Sugar: Â½ cup
  • Ghee/oil for deep frying

Method

  1. In a bowl, mix rice flour, mashed bananas, and sugar to make a smooth batter.
  2. Heat ghee/oil in a pan for deep frying.
  3. Pour a ladleful of batter in a circular motion into the hot oil to form a ring shape.
  4. Fry until both sides are golden brown.
  5. Remove from oil and drain excess oil on a paper towel.
  6. Serve Sel Roti warm with tea or as a snack.
