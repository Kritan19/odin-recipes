THUKPA-RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

  • Noodles (preferably flat or egg noodles): 200 grams
  • Chicken or vegetable broth: 4 cups
  • Boneless chicken or tofu: 250 grams, diced
  • Chopped onion: 1 medium-sized
  • Chopped garlic: 2 cloves
  • Chopped ginger: 1-inch piece
  • Chopped green chilies: 2
  • Sliced carrots: 1 cup
  • Sliced cabbage: 1 cup
  • Soy sauce: 2 tablespoons
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped cilantro for garnish

Method

  1. Boil the noodles separately according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a pot, heat some oil. Add chopped onion, garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Saut√© until aromatic.
  3. Add diced chicken/tofu and stir until it's lightly browned.
  4. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth. Bring it to a simmer.
  5. Add sliced carrots and cabbage. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  6. Season with soy sauce, salt, and pepper according to taste.
  7. In serving bowls, place a portion of boiled noodles
  8. Ladle the hot soup over the noodles and garnish with chopped cilantro.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy this comforting Nepali noodle soup.
