BUFFALO WINGS RECIPE

Ingredients:

  • Chicken wings: 2 lbs
  • Hot sauce (such as Frank's RedHot): ½ cup
  • Unsalted butter: 1/3 cup
  • Garlic powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Onion powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Paprika: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Method:

  1. Preheat your fryer or a pot of oil to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Pat dry the chicken wings with paper towels to remove excess moisture.
  3. In a saucepan, melt the butter over low heat. Add hot sauce, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir and simmer for a few minutes. Set aside.
  4. Fry the chicken wings in batches for about 10-12 minutes until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Transfer the fried wings to a bowl and toss them with the prepared sauce until evenly coated.
  6. Serve the Buffalo wings with celery sticks and blue cheese or ranch dressing for dipping.
  7. Enjoy your flavorful Buffalo wings!
