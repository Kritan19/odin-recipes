BARBECUE RIBS RECIPE

Ingredients:

  • Pork baby back ribs: 2 racks
  • Barbecue sauce: 2 cups
  • Brown sugar: 2 tablespoons
  • Garlic powder: 1 tablespoon
  • Smoked paprika: 1 tablespoon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method:

  1. Preheat your oven to 300째F (150째C).
  2. Remove the membrane from the back of the ribs and pat them dry with paper towels.
  3. In a bowl, mix brown sugar, garlic powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Rub the spice mixture over the ribs, covering them evenly.
  5. Wrap each rack of ribs tightly in aluminum foil and place them on a baking sheet.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 2.5-3 hours until the ribs are tender.
  7. Remove the ribs from the oven and increase the oven temperature to 425째F (220째C).
  8. Unwrap the ribs and place them on a new baking sheet lined with foil.
  9. Brush barbecue sauce generously over the ribs, ensuring they are well coated.
  10. Return the ribs to the oven and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the sauce caramelizes.
  11. Let the ribs rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
  12. Enjoy your delicious barbecue ribs!
