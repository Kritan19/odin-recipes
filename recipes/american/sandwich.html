SANDWICH RECIPE

Ingredients:

  • Bread slices: 4
  • Delicious fillings (ham, turkey, cheese, lettuce, tomato, etc.): as needed
  • Mayonnaise or mustard: as desired
  • Butter: for toasting (optional)
  • Any additional preferred toppings or spreads

Method:

  1. If desired, lightly toast the bread slices in a toaster or on a skillet with butter.
  2. Assemble the sandwich by placing the desired fillings (ham, turkey, cheese, lettuce, tomato, etc.) between the bread slices.
  3. Spread mayonnaise or mustard on one or both slices of bread, according to preference.
  4. Add any additional preferred toppings or spreads.
  5. Press the sandwich together gently to combine the ingredients.
  6. Slice the sandwich in half diagonally or as desired.
  7. Serve the sandwich immediately and enjoy!

Sandwich is Ready to Enjoy!!!
