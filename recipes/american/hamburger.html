HAMBURGER RECIPE

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef: 1 pound
  • Hamburger buns: 4
  • Cheddar cheese slices: 4
  • Lettuce leaves: 4
  • Sliced tomatoes: 4
  • Sliced onions: 4
  • Pickle slices: 8
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise: as desired
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method:

  1. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties. Season both sides of the patties with salt and pepper.
  2. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the patties for about 3-4 minutes per side for medium doneness or until desired doneness is achieved.
  3. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty and allow it to melt.
  4. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in the skillet until lightly browned.
  5. Assemble the hamburgers by placing a lettuce leaf on the bottom bun, followed by the cooked patty with melted cheese, sliced tomatoes, sliced onions, and pickle slices.
  6. Spread ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise on the top bun, if desired.
  7. Place the top bun over the assembled ingredients to complete the hamburger.
  8. Serve the hamburgers immediately while they're warm and enjoy!

Hamburger is Ready to Enjoy!!!
