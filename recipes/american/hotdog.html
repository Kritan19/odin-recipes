HOT DOGS RECIPE

Ingredients:

  • Hot dog sausages: 4
  • Hot dog buns: 4
  • Mustard: as needed
  • Ketchup: as needed
  • Relish: as needed
  • Chopped onions: as needed
  • Sauerkraut: as needed
  • Any additional preferred toppings

Method:

  1. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook the hot dog sausages on the grill or skillet, turning occasionally, until they are browned and heated through.
  3. Meanwhile, lightly toast the hot dog buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  4. Place the cooked sausages into the toasted buns.
  5. Add desired condiments and toppings such as mustard, ketchup, relish, chopped onions, sauerkraut, or any other preferred toppings.
  6. Serve the hot dogs immediately while they're warm and enjoy!

Hot Dogs are Ready to Enjoy!!!
