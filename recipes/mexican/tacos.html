TACOS RECIPE

Ingredients for Taco Seasoning:

  • Chili powder: 1 tablespoon
  • Ground cumin: 1 teaspoon
  • Smoked paprika: 1 teaspoon
  • Garlic powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Onion powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Dried oregano: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt: 1 teaspoon
  • Black pepper: ½ teaspoon
  • Cayenne pepper (optional for heat): ¼ teaspoon

Ingredients for Tacos:

  • Ground beef or turkey: 1 pound
  • Taco seasoning (as per the above ingredients): 2 tablespoons
  • Taco shells or tortillas: 8
  • Shredded lettuce: 1 cup
  • Diced tomatoes: 1 cup
  • Grated cheddar cheese: 1 cup
  • Diced onions: ½ cup
  • Diced avocado: 1
  • Sour cream: ½ cup
  • Salsa: ½ cup
  • Cilantro leaves for garnish
  • Lime wedges for serving

Method:

  1. Mix all the ingredients listed under taco seasoning in a small bowl and set aside.
  2. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef or turkey until browned. Drain excess fat if needed.
  3. Add the taco seasoning mixture to the cooked meat along with a little water (about ¼ cup) and simmer for 5-7 minutes until the liquid is absorbed and the meat is well coated.
  4. Warm the taco shells or tortillas according to package instructions.
  5. Assemble the tacos by placing the seasoned meat in the shells or tortillas.
  6. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, grated cheese, diced onions, diced avocado, sour cream, and salsa.
  7. Garnish with cilantro leaves and serve with lime wedges for squeezing over the tacos.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious tacos!

Tacos are Ready to Enjoy!!!
