MENUDO RECIPE

Ingredients:

  • Tripe (beef stomach): 2 pounds, cleaned and cut into small pieces
  • Pork shoulder or beef shank: 1 pound, cut into small cubes
  • Hominy (canned or dried and soaked): 2 cups
  • Water or beef broth: 10 cups
  • Onion: 1 large, chopped
  • Garlic cloves: 4, minced
  • Dried oregano: 1 tablespoon
  • Chili powder: 2 tablespoons
  • Bay leaves: 2
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lime wedges, chopped cilantro, chopped onion, and dried oregano for garnish

Method:

  1. Rinse the tripe thoroughly and cut it into small pieces. Place it in a large pot along with the pork shoulder or beef shank.
  2. Add water or beef broth to the pot and bring it to a boil. Reduce heat to a simmer and cook for about 2-3 hours until the tripe is tender.
  3. Skim off any foam that rises to the surface during cooking.
  4. Add hominy, chopped onion, minced garlic, dried oregano, chili powder, bay leaves, salt, and pepper to the pot.
  5. Continue to simmer the menudo for another hour or until the flavors are well combined and the meat is tender.
  6. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  7. Remove bay leaves before serving.
  8. Serve the menudo hot, garnished with lime wedges, chopped cilantro, chopped onion, and a sprinkle of dried oregano.
  9. Menudo is often enjoyed with warm tortillas or bread on the side.

Menudo is Ready to Enjoy!!!
