BURRITOS RECIPE

Ingredients for Burrito Filling:

  • Ground beef or shredded chicken: 1 pound, cooked and seasoned
  • Refried beans: 1 can (16 ounces)
  • Cooked rice: 1 cup
  • Shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend): 1 cup
  • Diced tomatoes: 1 cup
  • Chopped onion: ½ cup
  • Chopped fresh cilantro: ¼ cup
  • Sliced jalapeños (optional): ½ cup
  • Tortillas (large, flour): 6
  • Sour cream and salsa for serving

Method:

  1. Prepare the filling ingredients - cook and season the ground beef or chicken, warm up the refried beans, and have the cooked rice and other toppings ready.
  2. Warm the tortillas briefly in the microwave or in a skillet to make them pliable.
  3. On each tortilla, spread a layer of refried beans down the center.
  4. Layer the cooked meat, rice, shredded cheese, diced tomatoes, chopped onion, cilantro, and sliced jalapeños (if using) over the beans.
  5. Fold in the sides of the tortilla, then roll it up tightly to form a burrito.
  6. Repeat the process with the remaining tortillas and filling ingredients.
  7. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Place the burritos seam-side down and cook for a few minutes on each side until golden brown and crispy.
  8. Serve the burritos with sour cream, salsa, or your favorite toppings.
  9. Enjoy your delicious homemade burritos!
