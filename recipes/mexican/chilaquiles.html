CHILAQUILES RECIPE

Ingredients:

  • Corn tortillas: 12, cut into triangles
  • Vegetable oil: 2 tablespoons
  • Red or green salsa: 2 cups
  • Cooked chicken or beef, shredded (optional): 1 cup
  • Refried beans: ½ cup
  • Shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, or Mexican blend): 1 cup
  • Sour cream: ½ cup
  • Fresh cilantro leaves: for garnish
  • Sliced avocado: for garnish
  • Sliced jalapeños: for garnish (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Spread the tortilla triangles on a baking sheet and drizzle with vegetable oil. Toss to coat.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes until the tortillas are crispy and lightly golden. Remove from the oven and set aside.
  4. In a saucepan, heat the salsa over medium heat. Add the shredded chicken or beef (if using) and simmer for a few minutes until heated through.
  5. Spread a layer of refried beans on a serving platter or individual plates.
  6. Place the baked tortilla triangles over the beans, covering them evenly.
  7. Pour the salsa mixture over the tortillas, ensuring they are well coated.
  8. Sprinkle shredded cheese over the top of the chilaquiles.
  9. Bake in the oven for an additional 5-7 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Remove from the oven and garnish with dollops of sour cream, fresh cilantro leaves, sliced avocado, and sliced jalapeños (if using).
  11. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  12. Serve the chilaquiles hot and enjoy!

Chilaquiles are Ready to Enjoy!!!
