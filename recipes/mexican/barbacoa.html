BARBACOA RECIPE

Ingredients:

  • Beef chuck roast or brisket: 4 pounds, cut into large chunks
  • Chipotle peppers in adobo sauce: 2-3 peppers, minced
  • Garlic cloves: 4, minced
  • Cumin powder: 1 tablespoon
  • Dried oregano: 1 tablespoon
  • Apple cider vinegar: 1/4 cup
  • Beef broth or water: 1 cup
  • Bay leaves: 2
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh cilantro and lime wedges for garnish

Method:

  1. Preheat your oven to 275°F (135°C).
  2. In a blender or food processor, combine the minced chipotle peppers, minced garlic, cumin powder, dried oregano, apple cider vinegar, and a pinch of salt. Blend until it forms a paste.
  3. Place the beef chunks in a large oven-safe pot or Dutch oven. Season the beef with salt and pepper.
  4. Spread the paste mixture over the beef, ensuring it coats all sides of the meat.
  5. Pour beef broth or water into the pot and add bay leaves.
  6. Cover the pot with a lid and place it in the preheated oven.
  7. Braise the beef in the oven for 4-5 hours until the meat is tender and easily shreds with a fork.
  8. Once cooked, remove the pot from the oven and shred the beef using two forks.
  9. Discard the bay leaves and serve the barbacoa hot, garnished with chopped fresh cilantro and lime wedges.
  10. Barbacoa is commonly served in tacos, burritos, or as a main dish with rice and beans.

Barbacoa is Ready to Enjoy!!!
