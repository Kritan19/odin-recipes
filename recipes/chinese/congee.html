CONGEE RECIPE

Ingredients:

  • Rice: 1 cup, rinsed
  • Water or chicken broth: 8 cups
  • Salt: Â½ teaspoon
  • Optional Toppings:
    • Sliced green onions
    • Chopped cilantro
    • Fried garlic
    • Soy sauce
    • Sesame oil
    • Protein (chicken, pork, tofu, etc.)
    • Fried shallots

Method:

  1. In a large pot, bring the water or chicken broth to a boil.
  2. Add the rinsed rice to the boiling water or broth. Stir occasionally.
  3. Reduce heat to low and simmer the rice, stirring occasionally, for about 1 to 1.5 hours until the mixture thickens to a porridge-like consistency.
  4. Stir in salt and adjust seasoning to taste.
  5. Serve hot in bowls with your choice of toppings.

Congee is Ready to Enjoy!!!
