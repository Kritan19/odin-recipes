WONTON SOUP RECIPE

Ingredients for Wontons:

  • Wonton wrappers: 1 packet
  • Ground pork: ½ pound
  • Shrimp, peeled and deveined: ½ cup, finely chopped
  • Finely chopped green onions: ½ cup
  • Minced garlic: 2 cloves
  • Minced ginger: 1 teaspoon
  • Soy sauce: 2 tablespoons
  • Sesame oil: 1 teaspoon
  • Cornstarch: 1 tablespoon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Ingredients for Soup:

  • Chicken or vegetable broth: 4 cups
  • Chopped green onions for garnish
  • Soy sauce and sesame oil for seasoning (optional)

Method:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine ground pork, chopped shrimp, green onions, garlic, ginger, soy sauce, sesame oil, cornstarch, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  2. Place a teaspoon of filling in the center of each wonton wrapper. Moisten the edges with water, fold in half to create a triangle, and press the edges to seal.
  3. In a pot, bring the chicken or vegetable broth to a boil.
  4. Drop the wontons gently into the boiling broth and cook for 4-5 minutes until they float to the top and are cooked through.
  5. Remove the wontons with a slotted spoon and place them in serving bowls.
  6. Pour the hot broth over the wontons.
  7. Garnish with chopped green onions and season with additional soy sauce or sesame oil if desired.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!

Wonton Soup is Ready to Enjoy!!!
