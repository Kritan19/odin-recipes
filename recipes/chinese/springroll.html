SPRING ROLLS RECIPE

Ingredients:

  • Spring roll wrappers: 10-12
  • Shredded cabbage: 1 cup
  • Shredded carrots: 1 cup
  • Bean sprouts: 1 cup
  • Thinly sliced bell peppers: 1 cup
  • Thinly sliced tofu or cooked shrimp (optional): 1 cup
  • Rice vermicelli noodles, cooked: 1 cup
  • Chopped fresh cilantro: ¼ cup
  • Chopped fresh mint leaves: ¼ cup
  • Soy sauce: 2 tablespoons
  • Rice vinegar: 1 tablespoon
  • Sesame oil: 1 teaspoon
  • Oil for frying
  • Sweet chili sauce or plum sauce for dipping

Method:

  1. In a bowl, combine shredded cabbage, carrots, bean sprouts, bell peppers, tofu or shrimp (if using), cooked rice vermicelli noodles, cilantro, and mint leaves.
  2. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, rice vinegar, and sesame oil. Pour this mixture over the vegetable and noodle mixture and toss until well combined.
  3. Place a spring roll wrapper on a clean surface. Spoon about 2 tablespoons of the filling onto the bottom third of the wrapper.
  4. Roll the wrapper over the filling, folding the sides inwards, and continue rolling tightly to form a sealed spring roll. Use a bit of water to seal the edges if needed.
  5. Heat oil in a pan for frying. Fry the spring rolls in batches until they are golden brown and crispy. Drain excess oil on a paper towel.
  6. Serve hot with sweet chili sauce or plum sauce for dipping.

Spring Rolls are Ready to Enjoy!!!
