FRIED RICE RECIPE

Ingredients:

  • Cooked rice: 3 cups, preferably cold or leftover
  • Vegetable oil: 2 tablespoons
  • Eggs: 2, beaten
  • Chopped vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers, etc.): 1 cup
  • Chopped onion: 1 small
  • Minced garlic cloves: 2
  • Soy sauce: 2 tablespoons
  • Sesame oil: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper: ½ teaspoon
  • Spring onions: for garnish

Method:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Pour beaten eggs into the pan and scramble them. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add a bit more oil if needed. Sauté chopped onion and minced garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add chopped vegetables and stir-fry until they are slightly tender.
  5. Add cooked rice to the pan. Break up any clumps and stir-fry for a few minutes.
  6. Drizzle soy sauce and sesame oil over the rice. Add salt and black pepper. Mix everything well.
  7. Add the scrambled eggs back into the pan and toss with the rice until everything is heated through.
  8. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.

Fried Rice is Ready to Enjoy!!!
