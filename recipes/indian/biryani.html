BIRYANI RECIPE

Ingredients for Rice:

  • Basmati rice: 2 cups, soaked for 30 minutes
  • Cinnamon stick: 1 inch
  • Cardamom pods: 4-5
  • Cloves: 4-5
  • Bay leaves: 2
  • Salt to taste
  • Water: 4 cups

Ingredients for Chicken Masala:

  • Bone-in chicken: 750 grams, cut into pieces
  • Chopped onion: 2 large
  • Chopped tomatoes: 2 large
  • Ginger-garlic paste: 2 tablespoons
  • Green chilies: 2, slit
  • Red chili powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Turmeric powder: ½ teaspoon
  • Garam masala: 1 teaspoon
  • Fresh coriander leaves: a handful
  • Mint leaves: a handful
  • Yogurt: ½ cup
  • Lemon juice: 2 tablespoons
  • Oil: 4 tablespoons
  • Salt to taste

Method

  1. Cook rice with cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, bay leaves, salt, and water until it's 70% done. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add chopped onion and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and chopped tomatoes. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  4. Add chicken pieces, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, coriander leaves, mint leaves, yogurt, lemon juice, and salt. Cook until the chicken is almost done.
  5. In a heavy-bottomed pan, layer half of the partially cooked rice, followed by the cooked chicken masala. Repeat the layering.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes until the rice is fully cooked and aromatic.
  7. Serve hot with raita and salad.

Biryani is Ready!!!
