SAMOSA RECIPE

Ingredients for Outer Covering:

  • All-purpose flour: 2 cups
  • Carom seeds (ajwain): 1 teaspoon
  • Salt: ½ teaspoon
  • Oil: 4 tablespoons
  • Water: for kneading the dough

Ingredients for Filling:

  • Potatoes: 4 medium-sized, boiled and mashed
  • Green peas: ½ cup, boiled
  • Chopped onion: 1 large
  • Green chilies: 2, finely chopped
  • Ginger-garlic paste: 1 tablespoon
  • Cumin seeds: 1 teaspoon
  • Coriander powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Garam masala: 1 teaspoon
  • Red chili powder: ½ teaspoon
  • Turmeric powder: ¼ teaspoon
  • Fresh coriander leaves: a handful, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Method

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing all-purpose flour, carom seeds, salt, and oil. Knead it into a stiff dough using water. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. For the filling, heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onion, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste. Sauté until onions turn translucent.
  4. Add mashed potatoes, boiled peas, coriander powder, garam masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, fresh coriander leaves, and salt. Mix well and cook for a few minutes. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  5. Divide the dough into small balls. Roll out each ball into a small circle and cut it into halves.
  6. Take one half, make a cone shape, fill it with the potato-peas mixture, and seal the edges using water.
  7. Heat oil in a pan for deep frying. Fry the samosas on medium heat until golden brown and crispy.
  8. Drain excess oil on a paper towel and serve hot with chutney or sauce.

Samosa is Ready!!!
