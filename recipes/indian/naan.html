NAAN RECIPE

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour: 2 cups
  • Yogurt: ½ cup
  • Warm water: ½ cup
  • Sugar: 1 teaspoon
  • Active dry yeast: 2 teaspoons
  • Salt: ½ teaspoon
  • Butter or ghee: 2 tablespoons, melted
  • Garlic cloves: 2, minced (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves: 2 tablespoons, chopped (optional)

Method:

  1. In a bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and active dry yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  2. In another bowl, combine all-purpose flour, salt, yogurt, and the activated yeast mixture. Knead into a soft dough. Cover and let it rise for about 1-2 hours in a warm place.
  3. Preheat the oven to its highest temperature (usually around 500°F or 260°C) or use a pizza stone or cast-iron skillet in the oven while preheating.
  4. Divide the dough into equal portions and roll them into ovals or rounds about ¼ inch thick.
  5. If desired, sprinkle minced garlic and chopped coriander leaves on top of the naan and gently press them into the dough.
  6. Place the naan on a baking sheet or directly on the preheated pizza stone/skillet. Bake in the oven for 2-3 minutes or until puffed up and lightly browned.
  7. Remove from the oven, brush with melted butter or ghee, and serve hot.

Naan is Ready to Enjoy!!!
