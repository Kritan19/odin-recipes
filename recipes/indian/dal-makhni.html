DAL MAKHNI RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

  • Black lentils (whole urad dal): 1 cup
  • Red kidney beans (rajma): ¼ cup
  • Butter: 4 tablespoons
  • Chopped onion: 1 large
  • Chopped tomatoes: 2 large
  • Ginger-garlic paste: 2 tablespoons
  • Red chili powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt to taste
  • Garam masala powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Cream: ¼ cup
  • Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves): 1 tablespoon

Method

  1. Rinse and soak black lentils and red kidney beans separately for at least 4 hours. Pressure cook them together until soft.
  2. Heat butter in a pan. Add chopped onion and sauté until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  5. Add the cooked lentils and beans. Simmer on low heat for 20-30 minutes.
  6. Add cream and kasuri methi. Mix well and simmer for a few more minutes.
  7. Serve hot with naan or rice.

Dal Makhni is Ready!!!
