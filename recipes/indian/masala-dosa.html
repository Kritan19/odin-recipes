MASALA DOSA RECIPE

Ingredients for Dosa:

  • Parboiled rice: 2 cups
  • Split black lentils (urad dal): 1 cup
  • Fenugreek seeds: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking dosas

Ingredients for Potato Masala:

  • Potatoes: 4 medium-sized, boiled and mashed
  • Mustard seeds: 1 teaspoon
  • Cumin seeds: 1 teaspoon
  • Chopped green chilies: 2
  • Chopped onion: 1 large
  • Curry leaves: a few
  • Turmeric powder: ½ teaspoon
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped coriander leaves for garnish

Method

  1. For Dosa batter, rinse and soak rice, lentils, and fenugreek seeds separately for at least 4 hours. Blend them into a smooth batter, adding water as needed. Ferment overnight.
  2. Heat a flat non-stick pan. Pour a ladleful of batter and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin pancake. Drizzle oil around the edges. Cook until crisp.
  3. For Potato Masala, heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  4. Add chopped onion, green chilies, curry leaves, and sauté until onions turn golden.
  5. Add turmeric powder, mashed potatoes, and salt. Mix well. Cook for a few minutes.
  6. Place a spoonful of the potato masala in the center of the dosa, fold it, and serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.

Masala dosa is Ready!!!
