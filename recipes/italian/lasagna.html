LASAGNA RECIPE

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles: 12 pieces
  • Ground beef: 1 pound
  • Italian sausage: 1 pound
  • Onion: 1, finely chopped
  • Garlic cloves: 3, minced
  • Canned crushed tomatoes: 28 ounces
  • Tomato paste: 6 ounces
  • Dried basil: 2 teaspoons
  • Dried oregano: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Ricotta cheese: 15 ounces
  • Egg: 1
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese: 3 cups
  • Grated Parmesan cheese: 1 cup
  • Fresh parsley: 2 tablespoons, chopped (optional for garnish)

Method:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, Italian sausage, onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper to the skillet. Simmer for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix together ricotta cheese and egg until well combined.
  6. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of the prepared baking dish.
  7. Layer 4 lasagna noodles over the sauce. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles, then sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Repeat the layers: meat sauce, noodles, remaining ricotta cheese mixture, and cheeses.
  9. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  10. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes until bubbly and the cheese is melted and golden.
  11. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving. Garnish with chopped parsley if desired.

Lasagna is Ready to Enjoy!!!
