PIZZA RECIPE

Ingredients for Pizza Dough:

  • All-purpose flour: 2½ cups
  • Yeast: 1 packet (2¼ teaspoons)
  • Warm water: 1 cup
  • Olive oil: 2 tablespoons
  • Sugar: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt: 1 teaspoon

Ingredients for Pizza Toppings:

  • Pizza sauce: 1 cup
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese: 2 cups
  • Sliced vegetables (bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, etc.)
  • Sliced pepperoni or cooked sausage (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)
  • Additional toppings as desired

Method:

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. In a bowl, dissolve sugar in warm water. Sprinkle yeast over the water and let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  3. In a separate large bowl, mix flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil.
  4. Mix until the dough comes together, then knead on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  5. Form the dough into a ball, place it in a greased bowl, cover with a kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours until doubled in size.
  6. Punch down the dough and roll it out into a circle or rectangle on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  7. Transfer the rolled dough onto a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  8. Spread pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  9. Top with shredded mozzarella cheese and your choice of vegetables, meats, or additional toppings.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  11. Remove from the oven, sprinkle with fresh basil leaves (if using), slice, and serve hot.

Pizza is Ready to Enjoy!!!
