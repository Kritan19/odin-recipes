RAVIOLI RECIPE

Ingredients for Pasta Dough:

  • All-purpose flour: 2 cups
  • Eggs: 2
  • Olive oil: 1 tablespoon
  • Salt: ½ teaspoon
  • Water (if needed)

Ingredients for Filling:

  • Ricotta cheese: 1 cup
  • Grated Parmesan cheese: ½ cup
  • Chopped fresh spinach: 1 cup
  • Egg: 1
  • Garlic powder: ½ teaspoon
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Method:

  1. To make the pasta dough, mound the flour on a clean surface and make a well in the center. Add eggs, olive oil, and salt into the well.
  2. Slowly incorporate the flour into the wet ingredients using a fork or your hands until a dough forms. If the dough is too dry, add water, a tablespoon at a time, until it comes together.
  3. Knead the dough for about 8-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Wrap it in plastic wrap and let it rest for 30 minutes at room temperature.
  4. While the dough is resting, prepare the filling. In a bowl, mix together ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, chopped spinach, egg, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper until well combined. Set aside.
  5. Divide the rested dough into smaller portions. Roll out one portion of the dough into a thin sheet using a rolling pin or pasta machine.
  6. Place small spoonfuls of the filling on the dough sheet, leaving space between each spoonful.
  7. Place another sheet of dough over the filling and press around the edges of each ravioli to seal them.
  8. Use a knife or ravioli cutter to cut the individual ravioli.
  9. Cook the ravioli in boiling salted water for about 3-4 minutes until they float to the surface.
  10. Remove the cooked ravioli with a slotted spoon and serve with your favorite sauce and garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh herbs if desired.

Ravioli is Ready to Enjoy!!!
