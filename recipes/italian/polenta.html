POLENTA RECIPE

Ingredients:

  • Water: 4 cups
  • Cornmeal (polenta): 1 cup
  • Salt: 1 teaspoon
  • Butter or olive oil: 2 tablespoons (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese: Â½ cup (optional)
  • Freshly ground black pepper: to taste

Method:

  1. In a large saucepan, bring water to a boil.
  2. Gradually whisk in the cornmeal (polenta) in a slow, steady stream to avoid lumps.
  3. Reduce heat to low and cook the polenta, stirring frequently with a wooden spoon, for about 30-40 minutes until thickened and creamy.
  4. Add salt and continue to stir until well combined.
  5. Optional: Stir in butter or olive oil for added richness and flavor.
  6. Optional: Remove from heat and mix in grated Parmesan cheese for extra creaminess and a cheesy flavor.
  7. Season with freshly ground black pepper to taste.
  8. Serve the polenta as a creamy side dish or allow it to cool and set in a baking dish to be cut into slices and grilled or baked.

Polenta is Ready to Enjoy!!!
